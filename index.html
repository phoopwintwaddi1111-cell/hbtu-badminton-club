Hmawbi TU Badminton Club

Our Vision

  • To establish a highly capable and competitive badminton club that can proudly represent Technological University (Hmawbi).
  • To nurture not only competent future engineers and architects, but also skilled and accomplished badminton athletes.

Our Mission

  • To enhance the physical fitness and sporting abilities of students.
  • To organize and conduct regular training programs in a systematic and effective manner.
  • To support and nurture the development of skilled and competent badminton athletes.
  • To promote and strengthen the reputation of Technological University (Hmawbi) through participation and achievement in inter-university competitions.

Core Values

  • Commitment to Discipline
  • Respect for Others
  • Teamwork and Collaboration
  • Positive Attitude
